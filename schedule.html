Class Schedule

2017 - 2018


Classes are every Saturday, 8:45 - 12:30pm | Children's Introductory Class 8:45am - 12:15pm


For registration call (626) 964 - 5806

Spring Semester: February 3rd through June 16th, no school March 31st and May 26th
